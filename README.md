    Our game has our player character enter a turn based battle when coming in contact with the enemy on the overworld.
    We implemented the Attack and Heal player actions as simple ways to interact in combat.
    The enemy AI will chase the player on the overworld, and while in combat, will have a 20% chance to deal extra damage and heal itself while it is at below 50% health.
    Known issues: Currently, the animations in battle do not return to Idle once they are triggered.
